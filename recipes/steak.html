It's steak, frankly it needs no introduction...

Slab of meat, cooked to perfection

Steak is steak. I should not have to explain steak. Steak explains itself. If you don't know what steak is. Simply observe picture above. That is steak. That is all.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Kosher salt
  • Black pepper
  • 3 tablespoons canola oil
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves of smashed garlic
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Generously season steak on all sides with salt and pepper
  3. Transfer steak to wire rack, then bake for 45-60 minutes, until steak is cooked
  4. Heat the canola oil in a cast-iron skillet over high heat until smoking
  5. Sear the steak for on both sides then add all the other unadded ingredients into the pan and let it settle until satisfied
  6. Consume steak, it's just that simple!
